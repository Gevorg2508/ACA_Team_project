About the project

O2A was established in 2011 with the mission to help foreign
companies to outsource to Armenia a part of their accounting
services. In 2017 O2A introduced business consulting services to
its customers. In order to construct good relationships with
clients ant to give them the possibility to learn about the O2A
services it was decided to prepare a Website for the company.
