About us

O2A was established in 2011 with the mission to help foreign companies to outsource to Armenia a part of their accounting services. In 2017 O2A introduced
business consulting services to its customers.

Our international clients succeed with us thanks to the high quality of provided services at comparably low cost. Our local clients benefit from our qualified staff
and the practices that we gain during the collaboration with international and foreign companies.

Our company provides the overall management of the team here in Armenia. You will concentrate only on task delegation, as we manage HR, recruitment and
IT of your firm at the same time handling the required equipment and office space.

​Handling an offshore team will be very simple and easy. You will have the time to concentrate and provide more value to your business as we will be the ones to
keep track of the team's performance and efficiency.
